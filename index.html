About Us

Shamba harvest was founded in 2023 by Mary Wanjiku, a mama mboga at Marikiti Market. It is a platform where green grocer consumers are able to purchase goods at a relatively lower price compared to other platforms and the goods get delivered to their homes of preference or pick up station. Our dedication to excellence in both product quality and customer service is what sets us unique. We are aware that in today's fast-paced world, convenience is crucial. We are therefore happy to present our brand-new mobile web app, which was created to improve your purchasing experience.

We pride ourselves on offering a wide variety of seasonal fruits and vegetables at compettitve prices. Our produce is sourced directly from local farms and markets ensuring that you get the freshest and highest quality products available.




Our Mission
